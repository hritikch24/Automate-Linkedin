5 Amazing Science Facts

Fact 1: Sharks have existed for over 450 million years, predating dinosaurs, trees, and even Mount Everest.

Fact 2: A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis and 225 Earth days to orbit the sun.

Fact 3: Octopuses have three hearts, nine brains, and blue blood.

Fact 4: The average human produces enough saliva in their lifetime to fill two swimming pools.

Fact 5: Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly good to eat.
